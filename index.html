Our Technology and Vision

Our project is divided into several important steps:

Preparation: Planning and analyzing the building.
Scanning: Capturing the rooms with our cameras.
Data Processing: Creating the virtual tour.
Presentation: Releasing the tour to the public.

Our Project

For our project, we specialized in scanning the AI Makerspace in Tübingen. This building features modern technologies such as 3D printers, a podcast studio, a textile room, and is a central hub for education and technological innovation.

Details about the AI Makerspace

The AI Makerspace in Tübingen is a state-of-the-art, centrally located facility for technology and innovation

Our project aims to showcase the diverse possibilities and unique features of the AI Makerspace through a comprehensive 360-degree view.

About Us

We are a team of 8 dedicated students developing the "Hyperrealistic Indoor Street View" project. Our goal is to create software that enables realistic 360-degree interior views of buildings. By combining our strengths, we aim to provide an impressive virtual exploration experience.
